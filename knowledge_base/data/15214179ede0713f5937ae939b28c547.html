Creative writing with whiteboard and MS Word

The students work simultaneously with pen & paper and whiteboard to work with texts and do creative writing.

The students have a brief introduction in creative writing so that they can spot important parts of the sentences and write with that informations their own creative ending of the story. For this, they receive a worksheet and have time to read and mark it. Afterwards the teacher opens the same text in MS Word ob the whiteboard. Students and teacher are now working together on that file. Finally the students write their own ending of the story, based on the work they did before.

Material required: PC or Laptop, Whiteboard, internet, wireless mouse & keyboard, MS Word, 

Activities: The students have a brief introduction in creative writing. Afterwards they mark the important parts of the sentences on their paper. Finally they write a creative ending of the story on their on device

Organisation of the class: Whole class
