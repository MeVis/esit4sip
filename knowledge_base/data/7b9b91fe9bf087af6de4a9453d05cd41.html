First idea of gender

Trigger reflections about the attitudes that affect our sexual identity

Lesson Description: Help students to become aware/enhancing their awareness about issues related to gender.

Material required: Monitor, projector, computers

Activities: Students write down their "first thought" when they see a picture (e.g. Beyonce and Prince). Collected the thoughts on paper, compiled while students had a break and wrote up the results on the whiteboard. Then create short summary of what they have seen in the pictures (summary of the thoughts). Reflect on the different thoughts and the differences and similarities that exist. Connecting it to one's own life, to the general things and to professional role. At the end, students can create a short movie clip or a role play about what they learned in order to share with others.

Organisation of the class: Make sure the class is not divided as the discussion must be for the whole class together.
