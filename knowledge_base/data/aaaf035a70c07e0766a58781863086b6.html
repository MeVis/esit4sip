Five paragraphs Essay with online instructions

Combine textbook lesson and video lesson to explain and allow the creation of a five paragraphs essay.

Lesson Description: Have a brief review of an instruction in a textbook and then provide to students a movie clip to watch with similar information.

Material required: Computer and projector in the classroom. Enough computers/tablet for each students.

Activities: I start by going through the instructions for the essay in their textbook. I explain what benefit they may have to know the model that I go through. I go through and make sure that they know the English words that will appear in the movie clip. Students will listen to an online review of how to write a five paragraphs essay. When everyone understands what is expected of them, we go to the computer room where students write their texts (can work with tablets too).

Organisation of the class: Work with the whole class, on half a class if not enough computer/tablet available.
